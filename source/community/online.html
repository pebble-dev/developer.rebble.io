Online Communities

We have loads of online communities where you can interact with other Pebble developers, get help with your apps or just hang out and chat.

We have a great community of Pebble developers on Discord. Join us to chat about Pebble development!

Pebble Forums

The official Pebble forums are an excellent resource for developers to get help and advice on your Pebble apps and getting started with the SDK.

The forums can be found at {{ site.links.forums }} and you can use your Pebble username & password to login.

One of the best places to get help with Pebble development is on Stack Overflow using the pebble-sdk tag.

Hackster.io is the best place to learn about hardware electronics and show what you do as a maker. Discover and share DIY projects using your favorite platforms, including Pebble! Check out Pebble's projects page to see what hardware our community is building cool stuff with! You can even earn Pebbles through their hardware store.
